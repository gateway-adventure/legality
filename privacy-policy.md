\_Last Updated: 2025\_



This Privacy Policy explains how Gateway Adventure collects, uses, stores, and protects your data.

\## 2. How We Use Information

We use data to:

\- Improve user experience

\- Maintain security and prevent misuse

We do \*\*not\*\* sell personal data.

\- Required service providers (e.g., hosting, storage, authentication)

We use industry-standard security practices to protect personal data.  

No system is 100% secure, but we take all reasonable precautions.



\## 5. Your Rights

You may:

\- Request deletion of your data  

\- Request a copy of your stored information  

\- Opt-out of certain data collection features (where applicable)



\## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page.
